Autonomous Submersible Robot

PID-controlled syringe-pump buoyancy · Wi-Fi telemetry · 17 ft waterproof housing · Most Technical Award

Objective

Develop an autonomous submersible to measure underwater depth and capture imagery for cliff-diving safety.

Outcome

  • Fully autonomous descent/ascent using pressure-feedback PID control
  • Transparent PETG vacuum-formed shell with epoxy-sealed bulkheads
  • Wi-Fi logging of depth, temperature, and images upon resurfacing

Highlights

  • Depth-triggered failsafe and watchdog logic
  • Modular electronics bay with syringe-pump actuation
  • Validated to 17 ft depth; award-winning system integration

Tools & Skills

SolidWorks · ESP32 · C++ · MATLAB · Fluid Mechanics · PID Control
