Jumper Robot Clutch

Pawl clutch with Nitinol trigger · reliable release for untethered jumping

Objective

Design a compact, repeatable latch/clutch mechanism to store spring energy and release it quickly and reliably for a jumping robot.

Outcome

  • Consistent release behavior under high spring loads
  • Fast actuation using a Nitinol trigger wire
  • Improved repeatability across test runs through geometry and friction tuning

Highlights

  • Pawl geometry tuned for secure hold + clean disengagement
  • Low-friction contact surfaces and guided string path
  • Iterated through rapid prototypes and benchtop testing

Tools & Skills

SolidWorks · Mechanism Design · Prototyping · Testing
