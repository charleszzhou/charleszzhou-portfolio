Torsional Stiffness Measurement Apparatus

0.1 Nm/deg resolution · < ±5% variance · replaces a $12K torque sensor

Objective

Build a low-cost rig to quantify torsional stiffness of inflatable soft robotic beams.

Outcome

  • Pulley-based torque translation; bearing-mounted protractor
  • Swappable couplers; repeatable calibration across pressures
  • Validated stiffness–pressure trends

Highlights

  • Distinguishes subtle design differences in actuators
  • Modular, 3D-printed construction
  • Data analysis workflow for torque-angle regression

Tools & Skills

SolidWorks · 3D Printing · Mechanical Testing · Data Analysis
