About

I am a B.S. Mechanical Engineering student at UCSB (’26), specializing in Dynamical Systems & Controls and MEMS. My work sits at the intersection of embedded control, soft/robotic mechanisms, and microfluidic systems: building hardware, modeling its dynamics, and closing the loop with sensing and feedback so that real devices behave reliably despite nonlinearities and uncertainty.
